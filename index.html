Who We Are

A heart for the people, a vision for the future.

Our Story

CHLOES HEART INC was founded on the principle that no individual should walk through life's challenges alone. Based in Kuna, Idaho, we strive to be a beacon of hope for those in need.

Our Community

We believe in the power of local action. By focusing on our immediate neighbors, we create a ripple effect of kindness that extends far beyond Idaho.

Our Transparency

As a 501(c)(3) organization, we hold ourselves to the highest standards of financial integrity and public trust. Your support goes directly to our cause.

Our Mission & Impact

Our Core Philosophy

At CHLOES HEART INC, our mission is to provide comprehensive support services that empower individuals to reach their full potential. We believe that every person possesses inherent worth and deserves the opportunity to thrive in a safe, supportive environment.


Through our various programs, we address the root causes of instability and work tirelessly to provide resources, education, and emotional support. Our approach is holistic—viewing every challenge as an opportunity for growth and every individual as a vital part of our community tapestry.


What We Do

  • Community Outreach & Advocacy
  • Resource Navigation for Families
  • Educational Workshops and Support Groups
  • Crisis Intervention and Peer Support

Policies & Legal

Our commitment to your privacy and security.

Privacy Policy

CHLOES HEART INC respects the privacy of our donors and volunteers. We do not sell, share, or trade our donors' names or personal information with any other entity, nor send mailings to our donors on behalf of other organizations. This policy applies to all information received by CHLOES HEART INC, both online and offline.

Terms of Service

By accessing our website and participating in our programs, you agree to treat all members of our community with respect and dignity. CHLOES HEART INC reserves the right to refuse service to anyone who violates our code of conduct or engages in discriminatory behavior.

Donor Bill of Rights

Philanthropy is based on voluntary action for the common good. To assure that philanthropy merits the respect and trust of the general public, we declare that all donors have the right to be informed of the organization's mission and the way the organization intends to use donated resources.
